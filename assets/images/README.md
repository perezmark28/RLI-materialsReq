# Landing Page Images

**hero-bg.jpg** – Full-screen hero background on the landing page.
- Recommended size: 1920×1080 px or larger
- Format: JPG or PNG (JPG preferred for photos)

**logo.png** – Logo shown in the landing page header (RUDOLF LIETZ, INC. with RL monogram and “Sourcing Excellence”). Use a horizontal PNG.

**logo-icon.png** – Circular RL monogram (gold circle with R and L) used on the **login and signup** page (decorative panel and above the form on mobile). Use a square or circular PNG with black background.
